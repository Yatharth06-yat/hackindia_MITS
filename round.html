Online Open

Idea Submission Round

Participants submit their project idea along with the required documents for evaluation. The jury assesses innovation, feasibility, technical approach, presentation, and overall impact to shortlist teams for the Offline Grand Finale.

Judges Evaluate

  • Innovation
  • Problem Statement
  • Feasibility
  • Scalability
  • Technical Architecture

Submission Includes

  • Problem Statement & Solution PPT
  • 1–2 Minute Idea Video
  • GitHub Repository Link
  • Team Leader LinkedIn Profile
  • Prototype / Portfolio / Demo Links
  • Expected Impact
02
🏆
Offline Final Round

Grand Finale

Shortlisted teams compete in a 24-hour offline hackathon where they build, refine, and present their working solution before an esteemed panel of judges. The finale also features mentor sessions, networking opportunities, evaluation rounds, and the prize distribution ceremony.

Evaluation

  • Technical Execution
  • Execution
  • Presentation
  • Working Prototype
  • Impact

On the Day

  • Live Demo
  • Jury Judging
  • Networking
