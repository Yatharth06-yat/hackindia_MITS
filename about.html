ABOUT MITS

A Campus From
The Future

Madhav Institute of Technology & Science (MITS), Gwalior is a government-aided, autonomous institute founded in 1957 by His Highness Sir Jiwaji Rao Scindia — declared a Deemed-to-be-University under the Distinct Category by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, in 2024. Spread across a lush 45-acre campus that includes its own teak forest, "Madhav-Van," MITS is accredited NAAC A++ and ranks in the NIRF 251–300 Engineering band, offering 19+ undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across Engineering, Architecture, Management, and Computer Applications. Recruiters like Google, Amazon, Microsoft, TCS, Accenture, and Capgemini return year after year, and an AICTE-funded Centre of Excellence in AI & IoT anchors its research culture — making it the natural home for a national-level hackathon built on AI, Web3 & Blockchain, and Open Innovation.

ABOUT THE HACKATHON

IEEE HackVerse
MITS 2026

IEEE HackVerse MITS 2026 is a National-Level Online Hackathon organized by the IEEE Industry Applications Society (IAS) Student Branch Chapter and the IEEE Robotics and Automation Society (RAS) Student Branch Chapter, in collaboration with the IEEE Student Branch, MITS-DU Gwalior, and HackIndia. It challenges participants across multiple evaluation stages that test problem-solving ability, product thinking, UI/UX design, presentation skills, and technical expertise in Artificial Intelligence, Web3 & Blockchain, and Open Innovation — with responsible use of AI-powered development tools encouraged throughout.
